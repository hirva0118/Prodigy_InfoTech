Section 1

As the largest and most comprehensive online encyclopedia, it hosts over 58 million articles in more than 300 languages, with English alone boasting over 6.5 million entries. Operated by the non-profit Wikimedia Foundation, Wikipedia embodies the spirit of open editing, allowing volunteers worldwide to contribute and edit articles on a wide range of topics. It emphasizes the use of verifiable and reliable sources, making it a go-to resource for students, researchers, and curious individuals alike. Governed by a community of dedicated editors, Wikipedia remains a free, accessible, and multilingual platform, offering valuable information to millions across the globe.

Wikipedia is a free, open content online encyclopedia created through the collaborative effort of a community of users known as Wikipedians. Anyone registered on the site can create an article for publication; registration is not required to edit articles.
